all about instagrimy

robomex made this.

instagrimy pulls the last 200 restaurants that failed health inspection (roughly six weeks of failures) and then pulls any recent instagram media taken at those failing restaurants.

you can check out the health inspection fail reasons here. some of them are super gross.

instagrimy's code is all here.

instagrimy was made using:

  • meteor
  • city of chicago data
  • mapbox
  • leaflet
  • heap
  • google analytics
  • instagram
  • bootstrap
  • openstreetmap
  • facebook
